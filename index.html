Freelance Services Agreement
FreelanceKit Template · freelancekit.io

1. The Parties

This Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] between Freelancer: [YOUR FULL NAME OR BUSINESS NAME], located at [YOUR CITY, STATE/PROVINCE, COUNTRY], and Client: [CLIENT FULL NAME OR COMPANY NAME], located at [CLIENT ADDRESS], represented by [CLIENT CONTACT NAME, TITLE].

2. Project Scope & Deliverables

Freelancer agrees to provide the following services:

[DESCRIBE THE PROJECT IN SPECIFIC, MEASURABLE TERMS. E.G. "Design of 5 social media templates for Instagram (1080×1080px), delivered as editable Figma files and exported JPEGs. Includes 2 rounds of revisions."]

Deliverables:

[LIST EACH DELIVERABLE: • Deliverable 1: [Description, format] • Deliverable 2: [Description, format] • Deliverable 3: [Description, format]]
Scope note: Any work, deliverables, or revisions not listed above are outside scope and will require a written Scope Change Order with additional fees.

3. Timeline

Start: [START DATE] · Final delivery: [END DATE]. Timeline assumes Client provides materials and feedback within [3] business days of each request. Delays caused by late feedback extend the deadline accordingly.

4. Fees & Payment

Total fee: [AMOUNT AND CURRENCY]

  • [50%] deposit due on signing: [AMOUNT]
  • [50%] final payment due on delivery: [AMOUNT]

Invoices due within [7] days of receipt. Late balances accrue [1.5%]/month. Work does not begin until deposit clears. Final files withheld until final payment clears.

5. Revision Policy

Includes [2] rounds of revisions. Additional rounds billed at [YOUR HOURLY RATE]/hr. Feedback must be submitted consolidated within [7] days of delivery; late feedback treated as approval.

6. Kill Fee

  • Cancelled before work begins: deposit refunded in full.
  • Cancelled after work begins but before 50% completion: deposit forfeited.
  • Cancelled after 50% completion: full fee owed.

7. Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of full payment, Freelancer assigns all rights in final approved deliverables to Client. Until then, all work remains Freelancer's property. Source files, rejected concepts, and third-party assets are excluded unless explicitly listed as deliverables. Freelancer retains portfolio rights unless Client requests otherwise in writing.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information shared during this engagement and not to disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

9. Independent Contractor

Freelancer is an independent contractor, not an employee. Freelancer is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and equipment.

10. Dispute Resolution

Disputes will first be addressed in good faith within [14] days. If unresolved, disputes will proceed to binding arbitration in [YOUR CITY, STATE/PROVINCE] before either party pursues litigation.

11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [YOUR STATE/PROVINCE AND COUNTRY].
